Get to know me

I do more than sit at the computer

Ryan C. M. Mok

I am a freelance developer based in Chicago, IL., where I strive to learn and build as much as possible. My interest in coding came from a curiosity of how things are made and how they work; this lead me to pursue my certification in Full-Stack web development from Northwestern University in Evanston. I strive to continue learning because there's always something new and exciting that's being released into the wild; there's no limit to what you can learn and do if you have the desire.

If I'm not coding you can find me cooking, playing my sax, binging shows on Netflix, or blowing off some steam at the gym.
